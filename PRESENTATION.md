## Slide 3: The BMAD Process

An overview of our unique, multi-agent development process:

1.  **Phase 1: Planning** (Analyst, PM, Architect, UX)
    *   Generated PRD, Architecture, and UX Specs.
2.  **Phase 2: Execution** (SM, Dev, QA)
    *   Converted specs into code, stories, and tests.
3.  **Phase 3: Delivery** (All Agents)
    *   Finalized reports, documentation, and delivery.
